How to Backup iPhone Contacts Before Switching Phones

Switching iPhones? Before you reset, trade in, or replace your current phone, it is smart to save a separate backup of your contacts.

iCloud usually keeps contacts synced, but having your own exported contact file gives you an extra layer of protection. You can save the file, email it to yourself, upload it to cloud storage, or keep a copy for records.

Why Backup Contacts Before Switching Phones?

  • Keep a separate copy before resetting your old iPhone
  • Save contacts before trading in or selling your phone
  • Protect yourself in case iCloud sync is incomplete
  • Move contacts into spreadsheets or another contact system
  • Keep a simple record of names, numbers, and email addresses

CSV vs VCF

CSV

Best for spreadsheets, lists, cleanup, records, and reviewing your contacts in Excel, Google Sheets, or Apple Numbers.

VCF

Best for address books, contact transfers, and importing contacts into apps or systems that support vCard files.
