How to Export iPhone Calendar to Excel

Need to export your iPhone calendar to Excel? The easiest way is to create a CSV file from your calendar events, then open that file in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, Apple Numbers, or another spreadsheet app.

This guide explains how to export iPhone calendar events into a spreadsheet-friendly format so you can review, sort, filter, share, or save your calendar data.

Quick Answer

To export an iPhone calendar to Excel, export your calendar events as a CSV file. A CSV file can be opened directly in Excel and is useful for reports, backups, schedules, client records, invoices, time tracking, or personal archives.

How to Export iPhone Calendar to Excel

  1. Open Export Calendar - CSV & ICS on your iPhone.
  2. Select the calendar or calendars you want to export.
  3. Choose CSV as the export format.
  4. Create the export file.
  5. Save or share the CSV file.
  6. Open the CSV file in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or Apple Numbers.

Why CSV Works Best for Excel

Excel can open CSV files directly. CSV is a simple spreadsheet format that stores calendar event information in rows and columns, making it easier to review and organize your events.

If your goal is to analyze, sort, filter, print, or archive calendar data, CSV is usually the best export format.

CSV vs ICS for Calendar Export

CSV is best for Excel and spreadsheets. ICS is best for moving calendar events into another calendar app, such as Apple Calendar, Outlook, or Google Calendar.

If you want to open your iPhone calendar events in Excel, choose CSV.

Can iPhone Export Calendar to Excel Without an App?

Built-in iPhone calendar tools do not make it easy to export calendar events directly to an Excel-ready file. Some users try iCloud, desktop calendar apps, or manual copying, but those methods can take more steps and may require a computer.

A dedicated calendar export app is usually faster if you want a clean CSV file directly from your iPhone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I export my iPhone calendar to Excel?

Yes. Export your iPhone calendar events as a CSV file, then open the CSV file in Microsoft Excel.

What format should I use for Excel?

Use CSV. CSV files are spreadsheet-friendly and can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, Apple Numbers, and many other spreadsheet tools.

Can I export multiple iPhone calendars to Excel?

Yes. Export Calendar - CSV & ICS lets you select one or multiple calendars before creating your CSV file.

Does this work with Outlook calendars?

The app can export calendars that are available to the iPhone Calendar system. If an Outlook calendar is only inside the Outlook app and is not synced with the iPhone Calendar app, it may not appear as an available calendar to export.
