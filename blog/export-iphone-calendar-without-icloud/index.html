How to Export iPhone Calendar Without iCloud

Want to export your iPhone calendar without using iCloud? Apple Calendar on iPhone does not make calendar export very obvious, especially if you want a standalone file you can save, share, email, or open in another app.

This guide shows a simple way to export iPhone calendar events without relying on iCloud export tools, desktop workarounds, or manual copying.

Quick Answer

The easiest way to export an iPhone calendar without iCloud is to create a CSV or ICS file directly from your iPhone. CSV is best for Excel and spreadsheets. ICS is best for Apple Calendar, Outlook, Google Calendar, and other calendar apps.

CSV vs ICS: Which File Should You Choose?

Choose CSV for spreadsheets

CSV is the best choice if you want to open your iPhone calendar events in Excel, Google Sheets, Apple Numbers, or another spreadsheet app.

Choose ICS for calendar apps

ICS is the best choice if you want to import calendar events into Apple Calendar, Outlook, Google Calendar, or another calendar app.

Can You Export iPhone Calendar Without an App?

Built-in iPhone calendar tools are limited when it comes to creating standalone export files. Some workarounds use iCloud, desktop calendar apps, or manual copying, but those options can take more steps and may not give you the exact file format you need.
