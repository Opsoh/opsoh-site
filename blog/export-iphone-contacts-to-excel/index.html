How to Export iPhone Contacts to Excel

Need to export iPhone contacts to Excel? If you want to review, clean up, sort, or save your contact list in a spreadsheet, the easiest option is to export your contacts as a CSV file.

CSV files can be opened in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, Apple Numbers, and many other spreadsheet tools. This makes them useful for backups, contact cleanup, records, and simple contact management.

What File Format Should You Use?

CSV

CSV is the best choice if you want to open your contacts in Excel, Google Sheets, or Apple Numbers. It keeps your contact information in a simple spreadsheet-friendly format.

VCF

VCF is better if you want to import contacts into another address book, contact app, or device. It is commonly used for contact transfers and vCard-compatible systems.
