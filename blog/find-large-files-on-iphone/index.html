How to Find Large Files on iPhone

Large files can quietly take up a lot of storage space on your iPhone. Downloads, videos, PDFs, ZIP files, exported documents, and other saved files can accumulate over time without being obvious.

This guide explains how to find large files on iPhone, review file storage, and clean up unnecessary items to free up space.

Common Large Files on iPhone

  • Downloaded videos
  • Large PDFs and scanned documents
  • ZIP archives
  • Screen recordings
  • Exported files from apps
  • Duplicate or outdated documents

Why Large Files Matter

Even a small number of large files can consume significant iPhone storage. Removing old or unnecessary files may help free up space for photos, apps, updates, and backups.

Where Large Files Usually Come From

  • Safari downloads
  • Email attachments
  • Cloud storage downloads
  • Shared documents
  • Video editing exports
  • Messaging apps and transferred files

Simple Storage Cleanup Tip

Review files you no longer recognize or use. Old downloads and temporary files are often the easiest place to start when cleaning up storage.
