How to Export iPhone Reminders (Step-by-Step Guide)

Exporting iPhone reminders is not as simple as it should be. Apple’s Reminders app is great for managing tasks and lists, but it does not offer an easy built-in way to export them into a clean file. This guide shows a simple way to export your reminders for backup, sharing, or record keeping.

Quick Answer

There is no direct built-in iPhone option to export reminders into a simple file format. The easiest solution is to use a dedicated app that lets you select your reminder lists, export them quickly, and save or share the result.

Why Export iPhone Reminders?

  • Create a backup of your task lists
  • Share reminders with coworkers or family
  • Keep records of completed or active lists
  • Move tasks into another system or workflow

Manual Alternatives (Limitations)

Some users try copying reminders manually, taking screenshots, or moving tasks one by one into notes or spreadsheets. These approaches work in small cases, but they are slow, messy, and not ideal if you want a clean export.

When You Might Need to Export Your Reminders
