Why Save Contacts Outside of iCloud?

  • Keep your own separate copy of important contacts
  • Save contacts before changing phones, accounts, or devices
  • Export contacts for spreadsheets, cleanup, or record keeping
  • Share selected contact information with another person or team
  • Use a portable file instead of depending only on sync settings

CSV vs VCF

CSV

CSV is best when you want to view, sort, clean up, or store contacts in a spreadsheet. CSV files can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, and Apple Numbers.

VCF

VCF is best when you want to move contacts into another address book, contact app, or system that supports vCard files.
