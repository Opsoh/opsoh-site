FAQ

What is a VCF file?

A VCF (vCard) is a standard contact file format. Many apps and devices can import contacts from a .vcf file.

Is this really private?

Yes — your CSV is processed locally in your browser. No server upload. No storage.

How do I import VCF to iPhone?

Email the .vcf to yourself and open it on iPhone, or import to iCloud/Contacts from a computer. The exact steps vary depending on your workflow.
